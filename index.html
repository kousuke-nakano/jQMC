Welcome to jQMC#

jQMC code implements two real-space ab initio quantum Monte Carlo (QMC) methods. Variatioinal Monte Carlo (VMC) and lattice regularized diffusion Monte Carlo (LRDMC) methods.

The following features of jQMC are highlighted:

  • Variational Monte Carlo: Wavefuntion Optimization (VMC)

  • Lattice Regularized Diffusion Monte Carlo (LRDMC)

  • Atomic forces calculations by VMC and LRDMC

  • JAX-compaible modules

  • Interfaces with the TREX-IO wavefunction format
